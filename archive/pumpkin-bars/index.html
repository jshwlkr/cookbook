Pumpkin Bars

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 2/3 cup sugar
  • 1 cup cooking oil
  • 1 16 ounce can pumpkin
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda

Directions

Mix all ingredients together until well blended. Spread mixture on well-greased jelly roll pan, or cookie sheet with sides. Bake about 25–30 minutes at 350°F. Let cool. Spread cream cheese frosting on top.
